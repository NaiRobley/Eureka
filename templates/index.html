About Eureka

Eureka is an online platform where innovators post their ideas for the purpose of creating awareness.

Some ideas are conceived and never see the light of day due to certain factors like lack of support, or audience among other reasons.

This platform seeks to bridge that gap and give a voice as well as an audience to your ideas.

It creates a community of like-minded individuals who can help and contribute to the diverse set of projects on the platform

With your help, we will be able to build a global community of collaborating innovators
